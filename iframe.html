TO CONSIDER

What do you know about God

Have you ever taken a walk outside, in nature?

What are your favorite things to see?

There are trees and bushes, flowers and streams. Look in the sky. You can see the sun, the clouds, maybe even the top of a mountain. There are wonderful animals all around: birds, and insects, and lizards, and lots more. The world is full of amazing, beautiful things.

Have you ever wondered where they all came from?

All those things you see,

in fact the whole world,

came from the same

Person – God.

Who is God?

God is the Creator. He made the entire world, and everything in it. He made all the mountains and oceans, all the animals and plants; He even made the stars and other planets.

Most importantly, God made all people, including you

What are some things we know about God?

One is that God is eternal. This means that He has always existed. God does not have a beginning. Before anything else existed, there was only God. God is all-powerful. That means that God can do anything. There is nothing that God does not have the power to do.

God is also all-good. God is perfect, and everything He does is perfectly good. God is also everywhere. Even though we may not always realize it, God is always there, and He knows everything. God sees everything we do, and He even knows what we think

Any time you want to talk to God, you can. He always hears you

Holy Heroes

St. Francis of Assisi

St. Francis was born in 1181 in the town of Assisi in Italy. When he was young, he wanted to be a knight. But Francis heard God calling him to something greater. He fell in love with God and wanted to follow Him with all his heart. Francis started the religious group known as the Franciscans. Francis loved all of God’s creation. He saw God’s beauty and love in everything He had made. More than anything, Francis wanted to be holy and to please God. He died in 1226. St. Francis of Assisi’s feast day is October 4.

St. Francis of Assisi, pray for us!
